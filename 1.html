OUR SERVICES

Decontamination

Our biodegradable chemical application eliminates the hazards of dangerous gases such as Benzene, H2S, LEL, and other elements inside process equipment within 12-16 hours. This not only reduces shutdown and turnaround duration but also increases profitability and enhances health and safety performance. The risk of falling behind schedule is completely eliminated.

Chemical Cleaning

By utilizing a minimal footprint, biodegradable chemicals, and employing professional application methods, we ensure that your equipment is completely safe for hot work and entry. Within a span of 12-16 hours, our process effectively eliminates all sludge, dangerous solids, residual oil, and hazardous gases. This guarantee of a maximum safety level not only eliminates the risk of human exposure to hazardous gases but also saves valuable time in your overall shutdown schedule.

Trading

As part of our trading services, we excel in importing and supplying specialized industry chemicals, catering to a wide spectrum
of sectors. With our strong partnerships with global manufacturers, we ensure a seamless supply, delivering high-quality chemicals that meet the stringent industry standards. We are being a reliable source for Personal Protective Equipment (PPE) supplies. From flame resistance clothes to other essential safety gear.

Contracting

With our team of skilled professionals and extensive industry knowledge, we specialize in delivering turnkey projects that encompass
various aspects of industrial operations. From equipment installation and maintenance to facility upgrades and construction, we have the expertise to handle projects of all sizes and complexities. Whether you require a complete plant or specific infrastructure, our contracting services are tailored to meet your requirements.

Technical Support

As part of our technical support services, we specialize in providing chemical solutions that optimize operational efficiency and enhance productivity. Whether you require guidance on selecting the right chemicals for your processes, assistance with troubleshooting and process optimization, or access to innovative chemical technologies, our technical support team is here to help.

ABOUT US

Cidra Sharq Trading & Contracting Company was established in 2012 by a team of experienced engineers with a successful track record in managing major projects. The company has undertaken various civil, mechanical, and electrical projects across the Eastern Province of Saudi Arabia.

  • As part of its expansion, Cidra Sharq has diversified into the oil and gas industrial services sector, focusing on chemical cleaning, decontamination, and degassing of hazardous gases such as LEL, benzene, and H2S. These services have become the core focus of Cidra Sharq’s operations.
  • Cidra Sharq proudly serves as the exclusive distributor and technical partner of Aztron Chemical Services Company, headquartered in Houston, USA. This partnership enables Cidra Sharq to offer a comprehensive range of decontamination and degassing chemicals, as well as H2S scavengers and degreasers.
